Our Story

In 2018, Phidelia moved into Teshie Ward 1 of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Despite being in the same ward, we were never close friends and rarely spoke. While I admired Phidelia, I never had thoughts of marrying her.

Fast forward to 2020, during one of my most challenging moments, I decided to take a chance and express my feelings to her. However, my attempt was unsuccessful as she was already in a relationship.

Then, in 2023, something unexpected happened. Phidelia joined Telegram, and I received a notification, as I typically did with my contacts. Curious, I inquired about her presence on Telegram. She responded, but unfortunately, I didn't receive her message until days later. Nevertheless, this sparked the beginning of our conversations.

As we continued chatting, I could have never foreseen this moment, ready to embark on this shared path. Phidelia embodies everything I ever hoped for in a woman – she's extraordinary in every way imaginable, and I feel incredibly blessed to have her by my side.

Hope you enjoyed our short story. Kindly leave a note in our guest book.

Whether you're joining us on our wedding day or stumbling upon this message days later, we're thrilled to have you here. Life has a funny way of bringing unexpected connections, doesn't it?

Not long ago, we were just strangers in the same ward, barely exchanging a word. Then fate intervened, and our online chats brought us closer together. It's as if we found missing pieces of ourselves in each other, making our bond stronger than ever.

This marks the beginning of our journey as a married couple, and we couldn't be more excited. With Phidelia by my side, I feel like I can conquer anything. Together, we're ready to embrace all the adventures life has in store for us.

So, whether you're here to witness our vows or catch up later, we invite you to share your thoughts in our guest book. Your words will always hold a special place in our hearts as we embark on this new chapter together.
