Our Story

In 2018, Phidelia moved into Teshie Ward 1 of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Despite being in the same ward, we were never close friends and rarely spoke. At that time, I served as the clerk under Bishop Owoo. While I admired Phidelia, I never entertained thoughts of marrying her.

Fast forward to 2020, during one of my most challenging moments, I decided to take a chance and express my feelings to her. However, my attempt was unsuccessful as she was already in a relationship.

Then, in February 2024, something unexpected happened. Phidelia joined Telegram, and I received a notification, as I typically did with my contacts. Curious, I inquired about her presence on Telegram. She responded, but unfortunately, I didn't receive her message until days later. Nevertheless, this sparked the beginning of our conversations.

As we continued chatting, I never imagined that we would eventually find ourselves standing before all of you, ready to exchange vows. Phidelia is an extraordinary woman, exactly the type and kind I had always prayed for.

We hope you enjoyed our short story and kindly leave a note in our guest book.
